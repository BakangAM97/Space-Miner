Space Miner

Welcome to Space Miner

Use the arrow keys to fly around space and get to the safe zones before they disappear, collecting points along the way. If you don't make it in time you lose a life. The aim of the game is to collect as many points as you can before you run out of lives. Good Luck.

Entering the safe zone will get you 1 point

Collecting coins will gain you 2 points

Collecting hearts will gain you 1 extra life

Easy Mode:

In this mode attaining 5 lives will cause money bags to start spawning money bags. If you get the bag before it disappears you will be rewarded 5 points

Hard Mode:

In this mode you don't get any extra lives, however you will be able to move faster and money bags will start spawning from the start of the game.
